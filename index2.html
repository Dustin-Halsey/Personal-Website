Welcome!

Welcome to the personal webpage of Dustin Halsey! I am a computer game deisgner who recently graduated from University of California, Santa Cruz with a Bachelor of Science in "Computer Science: Computer Game Design". Fueled by my lifelong passion towards games, I began developing video games Sophomore year of high school and have been creating games and improving my skills ever since.

     As a lifelong gamer and a tinkerer at heart, I crave the satisfaction that comes with getting down to the nitty gritty of how things work and using that knowledge to construct something that I can truly call my own. While primarily focusing on the programming side of things, I make it my mission to be well versed in all aspects of the game design process including 3D modeling, animation, sound design, and more.

     Here, you can find my résumé and a portfolio of my past projects as well as a collection of side projects i've created for fun. If you'd like to get in touch, I'd love to chat!
